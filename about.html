How It Works

From your heart to a child's hands — here's how

We believe you deserve to know exactly how your donation travels from your pocket to the children who need it most.

The Process

Your donation's journey

1

You Give

Choose your amount and frequency. Your payment is processed securely via Flutterwave using bank-grade encryption. One-time or monthly — every contribution matters.

2

We Allocate

Within 24 hours, funds are reviewed by our programmes team and directed to the highest-need areas via our network of 14 vetted local partners across 8 countries.

3

Partners Receive

Our local partners — doctors, teachers, community leaders — receive funds and deploy them immediately. They know the terrain, the language, and the families personally.

4

Children Receive

Food, medicine, shelter, education, and emotional support reach children within days of your donation. Every item is documented with photos and receipts.

5

You're Updated

Monthly impact reports land in your inbox. Photos, stories, and financial breakdowns show you exactly how your generosity made a difference. Full transparency, always.

FAQ

Questions we hear often

Is my donation tax deductible?

Yes. Hope for the Children is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit (EIN: 12-3456789). All donations are fully tax deductible in the US. A receipt will be emailed immediately after your donation.

How do I know my money reaches children?

We publish monthly impact reports with photos, field notes, and financial breakdowns. Our annual accounts are independently audited and publicly available. 94¢ of every dollar goes directly to programmes.

Can I cancel my monthly donation?

Absolutely. You can cancel or pause your monthly giving at any time by contacting us at giving@hopeforthechildren.org. There are no fees or penalties.

What currencies do you accept?

We accept USD, GBP, EUR, NGN, CAD and many more via Flutterwave. You can also pay by card, bank transfer, mobile money, and USSD.

Can my company make a corporate donation?

Yes — we welcome corporate partnerships and gift matching. Contact us at partners@hopeforthechildren.org to discuss how your company can get involved.
