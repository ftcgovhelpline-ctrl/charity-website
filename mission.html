Our Mission

Bringing hope to children in the heart of conflict

Since 2018, we have worked tirelessly to deliver life-saving aid and long-term support to children across Iran and the wider Middle East.

What We Do

Where your generosity goes

Food & Clean Water

Daily nutritious meals and clean drinking water for children suffering from malnutrition and displacement in conflict zones. Our food parcels reach families within 48 hours of a crisis.

4,200+ Children fed daily

Medical Care

Emergency healthcare, trauma treatment, vaccinations, and ongoing support for injured and sick children. Our mobile clinics reach areas with no hospital access.

2,800+ Medical treatments provided

Education & Shelter

Safe learning environments, temporary housing, and school supplies so children can rebuild their lives and look toward the future. Education is hope.

3,100+ Children in our schools

Mental Health Support

Trauma counseling, play therapy, and psychosocial support to help children heal from the invisible wounds of war. Because healing goes beyond the physical.

1,900+ Children in therapy

Our Principles

What we stand for

01

Transparency

Every dollar is tracked and reported. Our annual audits are publicly available and we share field reports monthly with all supporters.

02

Local-First

We work through vetted local partners who understand the culture and context — not outsiders imposing solutions from afar.

03

Child-Centred

Every decision we make starts with one question: what is best for the child? Their dignity, safety and future come first.
