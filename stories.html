Featured Story

Maryam's journey back to school

At just 8 years old, Maryam had already experienced more upheaval than most adults. Displaced from her home in southern Iran, she and her family arrived in Tehran with nothing.

For two years, she hadn't set foot in a classroom. The trauma of displacement had left her withdrawn and fearful. Then our education team found her family.

"The first day she came home with a drawing she made at school, I cried. I had not seen her smile like that in two years."

— Maryam's mother, Tehran

Today, Maryam is in second grade, learning to read and dreaming of becoming a doctor. Your donations made this possible.
